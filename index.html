How to Use LeadHunt
A step-by-step workflow for finding and closing web design clients — built for Pdified
STEP 1
Find Prospects with the Niche Browser
Go to 📋 Niche Browser — 50 underrated business types are listed, each with a reason why they need a website and their typical job ticket size.

Filter by category (Home Exterior, Automotive, B2B, etc.) or search for a specific type.

Click any niche card → it pre-fills the industry field in a new lead form automatically.
STEP 2
Pick a High-Yield Region
Go to 🗺 Region Browser — 50 wealthy, underserved markets across USA, Canada, UK, and Australia.

These are secondary markets with high household incomes and very few competing web agencies — ideal cold outreach targets.

Click any region card → pre-fills the city and country in your lead form.
STEP 3
Generate Search Queries
Go to 🔍 Search Queries — pick a niche and a region.

The tool generates 10 targeted Google / Google Maps search strings you can copy and paste directly to find businesses with ugly or missing websites.

Pro tip: Also use Ugly Site Scraper ($29–$59/mo) to automate this — it scans Google Maps and scores website quality automatically, then export the CSV.
STEP 4
Add Leads & Score Their Opportunity
Click + Add Lead and fill in what you know — name, phone, city, website URL, and tick all the issues you spot.

Opportunity Score (0–100) is calculated automatically:
🔥 80–100 = Hot — contact immediately 🌤 50–79 = Warm — worth pitching 🧊 0–49 = Cold — lower priority
Higher scores = more issues found = bigger opportunity for you. No website scores highest.
STEP 5
Send the WhatsApp Pitch
Click any lead card to open its detail panel.

A personalised pitch message is auto-generated based on the business name, location, and the specific issues you ticked. It's written in your name (Adewumi — Pdified).

Click 📋 Copy Message then click Open in WhatsApp → to send directly.

Pro tip for Nigeria: WhatsApp cold outreach converts far better than email for Lagos/Abuja SMEs. Send between 9am–12pm weekdays.
STEP 6
Track & Follow Up
Use the status pipeline to track every lead:
NEW → just added, not yet contacted CONTACTED → pitch sent, awaiting reply FOLLOW-UP → interested, needs more info or proposal CLOSED ✓ → deal won
Use the Notes field to log call times, what was discussed, and next actions.

Click ⬇ Export CSV at any time to download all your leads to a spreadsheet.
💡 Pro Tips for Hitting 500 Leads Fast
✅ Use Ugly Site Scraper — $59/mo Ugly Plan gives 600 credits. Run 10 niche + city combos = 500+ leads in one afternoon. Export CSV.
✅ Target Lagos niches first — Restaurants, Clinics, Schools, Churches, Event Centres. Massive volume, easy WhatsApp access.
✅ For international clients — use the Region Browser. Williamson County TN, Muskoka ON, and Loudoun County VA are goldmines with almost zero local web agencies.
✅ Sort by Highest Score — always pitch 🔥 Hot leads first. These are businesses with the most problems = easiest to convince.
✅ Follow up 3x — most clients respond on the 2nd or 3rd message. Use the Notes field to track when you last contacted them.
✅ Data persists in your browser — LeadHunt saves everything locally. Use the same browser/device for continuity. Export CSV regularly as backup.
